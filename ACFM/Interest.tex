\subsection{Compund Interest}

\begin{description}
\item[A] Accrual at year $n$

\[
A_n+iA_n = A_{n+1} \mbox{ for }(n \geq 1)
\]

Accumalation of C for n time unites under compound interest at rate i per unit time

\[ C(1+i)^n\]
