example 3.c.3.2 Credit Card Operational Risk

Question
50,000 Credit Cards will be in issuance over the forthcoming year.
The expected Probability of an incidence of Credit Card Fraud is 0.05.

If every frauds gives rise to an operational loss of $1000

a) Calculate the Expected Loss
b) The ORC at the 99.9% percentile, with and without the assumption that the expected loss
is already covered by the normal cost of business.

Solution

Expected Loss lambda = 2500
Loss - NPLx = 50000 x 0.05 x 1000 = 2.5 million

ORC = gamma expected annual loss
