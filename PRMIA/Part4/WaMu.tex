\section{Washington Mutual}

The failure of  Washington Mutual as a savings and loan association  became the largest bank failure in U.S. history.

The United States' largest savings and loan association until it became the largest bank failure in U.S. history.

Washington Mutual Bank's closure and receivership is the largest bank failure in American financial history.

Before the receivership action, it was the sixth-largest bank in the United States.
 
According to Washington Mutual Inc.'s 2007 SEC filing, the holding company held assets valued at $327.9 billion.
