\subsection{Balance Sheet}

Accounting Equation
\[ \mbox{Assets} = \mbox{Libailities} + \mbox{Equity}\]

\begin{description}
\item[Assets] Fixed Assets, Current Assets
\item[Libailities] Current Liabilities, Long Term Borrowing, Issued Share Capital, Capital Reserves, REvenue Reserves
\end{description

\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Fixed Assets}  - tangible, intangible, investment
\item \textbf{tangible} - Deprectiaton (Straigt line Method or Reducing Balance Method) . Useful Economic Life

\item \textbf{intangible fixed assets} - Goodwill, purchased goodwill (price paid for company - fair value for seperable net assets)

\item \textbf{Invesments} - Invesment held in other companies

\end{itemize}
